hrvstr Widget Demo

Embeddable short-form feed with Google Ad Manager integration

Live Widget Demo

Click the widget below to open the lightbox feed. Navigate using arrow keys (desktop) or swipe up/down (mobile). Press ESC to close.

How It Works

  • Feed Rotation: First session of the day starts with Recent tier. Subsequent sessions rotate through Popular and Evergreen.
  • Companion Ads: 320x50 banner appears on every content card
  • Full Ad Cards: Inserted every 4 content cards, showing VAST video (with skip) or display fallback
  • Analytics: Events are batched and sent to backend every 3 seconds
  • Navigation: Desktop: arrow keys and buttons. Mobile: swipe gestures
